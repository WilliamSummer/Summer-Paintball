Paintball information

What is paintball?

Paintball is a combination of the games tag and dodgeball. Each player is equiped with a paintball marker and a paintball mask. The objective of the game is to shoot opposing players with a paintball marker. Matches last anywhere from 5 minutes to 20 minutes. Depending on the size of the field and the amount of players.

Safety rules

During a paintball match, the paintball mask is required to stay on your face until the match is completed. The mask is required for your protection. When a match is not going, a barrel sleeve is required to be on the end of the paintball marker. This is to protect you and others from accidental discharge.

Gameplay rules

Paintball begins when the announcer says go. To win, you must either eliminate the oposing team or complete the objectives.
